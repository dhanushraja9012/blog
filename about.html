This village is known for the cult of God Muniyandi, basically known as caretaker of the village and its people. Every year a grand festival is organised by the village origin to workship the Muniyandi. People who are part of this village residing all over Tamil Nadu will come on third week of every January month and they pay their workship and respect to the renowned god Muniyandi. The family business for the people of village and its origin are running a hotel in the name of "Madurai Sri Muniyandi Vilas", a renowned non-vegetarian hotel famous in Tamil Nadu.
